--- layout: post title: gfxCardStatus v1.5 - Dependent Process List! published: true ---

The latest version of gfxCardStatus has been released! It now has a list of processes that are using the NVIDIA card, if that's the active GPU. Now it's easier than ever to tell exactly what's the culprit of your shortened battery life.

As usual, just hit "Check for Updates" from the menu bar icon if you've got v1.2 or above already, or go download it at http://codykrieger.com/gfxCardStatus/
